Inspiration

As students, we struggled with the rapid turnover of property listing on Durham estate agents websites. We aimed to make a solution that: - Unites buyers and sellers, - Is ergonomic and accessible, - Gives customers peace of mind that they will never miss out on their dream home,

What it does

Our website is for both people looking to buy properties and people selling properties. Buyers can sign up for notifications for all of the properties on the market. Sellers can get simple, accurate property valuations at the click of a button. Through this unified website, we can unite buyers and seller to streamline the process of property transactions.
